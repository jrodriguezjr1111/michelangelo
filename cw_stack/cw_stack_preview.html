Design checks

    Print every part floor-down, no supports (groove and csk are on the bed face). Clamp: one length of M3 from the lid's csk top threads down into the ZED base's heat-set inserts — add a module = a longer bolt. Per-layer inserts (each tray bolts to the one below) are the serviceable alternative. Tongue/groove FIT is the same PLA-tuned snug as the box coupon.
