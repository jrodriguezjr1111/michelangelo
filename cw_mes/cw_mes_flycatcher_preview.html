CW-MES — Nooelec FlyCatcher box OpenSCAD

Same modular 6-panel bolted enclosure as the Jetson Nano box (shared cw_mes_lib.scad) — only the parameters change. The FlyCatcher is two radios; each radio's SMA + USB routes out its own side wall through a CW-Link cable gland (4 bores total). Board sits on bottom-plate riser bosses. Source: cw_mes_flycatcher.scad.

Design checks

    Confirm vs calipers: board size, mount-hole pattern, gland heights, insert Ø (M3 vs M2.5).

    Print each panel flat, no supports (PLA clr 0.10, coupon-verified). Glands are the CW-Link parts — M18 here is generous; drop GLAND_D and the box shrinks with it (the gland nut sets the X clearance). Panels interchange with the rest of the CW-MES family (matching edge lengths + tab counts).
