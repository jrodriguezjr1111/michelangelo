GNSS Saddle Enclosure

Enclosure that hangs under the +Y wing of gnss_saddle/make_gnss_saddle_wide.py. Mount face is the top of the enclosure; cavity opens downward. Houses the ZED-F9P breakout, with USB-C + Ø6.3 GNSS antenna passthroughs on the −X wall. Source: gnss_saddle/make_gnss_enclosure.py

Design checks

    STL is pre-flipped for printing — floor-down on the bed, cavity-up. No supports. In service, the part flips: mount face is up against the saddle, cavity opens toward the ground. Press M3 nuts into the floor pockets from below, then drop bolts down through the saddle's countersunk ZED holes to tighten.
