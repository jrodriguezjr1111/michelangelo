Enclosure that hangs under the +Y wing of gnss_saddle/make_gnss_saddle_wide.py. Mount face is the top; cavity opens downward. Houses the ZED-F9P breakout. Design checks

    STL is pre-flipped for printing — floor-down on the bed, cavity-up. No supports. In service, the part flips: mount face is up against the saddle, cavity opens toward the ground. Drop M3 bolts down through the saddle's countersunk ZED holes; fasten with free nuts/washers or heat-set inserts as preferred.
